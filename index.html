Benefits of a Maker Space?

Every makerspace is unique & the projects that are worked on inside of them are also very diverse. Here are just some of the things you can do in a makerspace :

  • Coding
  • 3D Scanning & 3D printing
  • Laser cutting
  • Soldering
  • Electronics / Arduino
  • Robot building / Robotics
  • Learn Circuits & Electricity with paper circuits

Benefits of an Internet Cafe?

Internet Cafes at their core, are a place that allow equal opertunity to people to access the internet. By proxy then have access to ways to improve their station in life with:

  • News & Current Events
  • Education & e-Learning Solutions
  • Create & Program
  • Write & publish
  • Of Course; Games
